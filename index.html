The Geometry of Being

Have you ever poured yourself into everyone else and arrived home empty?
Been so fixed on the destination that you missed everything along the way?
So certain you were right that the conversation ended before it began?

Each of those is a shape out of balance.
The Geometry of Being is the practice of holding all three.

Circle. Triangle. Square. The WholeShape.
A practice of integration, rooted in the body.

The Geometry of Being Project — situated at Aikido of Champlain Valley, Burlington, Vermont

This framework emerged from Aikido — a martial art built on the premise that conflict can be met without opposition. Circle, triangle, and square describe how to move. The Geometry of Being explores what it means to carry all three at once, not one at a time, as a single quality of being.
