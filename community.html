The Idea

Built by more than one person

Circle, square, triangle didn't stay on the mat. The hope is that they won't stay on this website either.

Right now, this framework lives through a handful of people — an Aikido teacher, a writer, a shape-maker. But the three shapes aren't specific to Aikido. They show up in how a nurse handles a hard shift, how a parent sets a boundary, how a team ships a project under pressure. Anyone applying circle, square, and triangle to their own work or life is already part of this.

The plan is to build a place where people can share how they're using the framework in their own sector — business, healthcare, education, art, parenting, sport, whatever it is — and learn from how others are using it in theirs.

A collective of people, applying the same three shapes, in completely different rooms.

What We Want to Build

A Forum

A space to discuss how the framework shows up in real situations — ask questions, share what's working, push back on what isn't.
