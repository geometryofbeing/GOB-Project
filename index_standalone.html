The Geometry
of Being

Have you ever poured yourself into everyone else and arrived home empty?
Been so fixed on the destination that you missed everything along the way?
So certain you were right that the conversation ended before it began?

Each of those is a shape out of balance. The Geometry of Being is a framework for holding all three.

Circle. Triangle. Square. The WholeShape.
A practice of integration. A way of moving through life.

The Geometry of Being Project — situated at Aikido of Champlain Valley, Burlington, Vermont
